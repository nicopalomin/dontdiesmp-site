Privacy Policy

Last updated:

What we collect

We collect only the information you submit in the whitelist application form (like your Minecraft username). Discord/Reddit/email are optional if you provide them.

How we use it

  • To review and approve/deny whitelist applications
  • To contact you if you provided contact info (optional)
  • To prevent spam/abuse (anti-bot checks)

What we don’t do

  • We don’t sell your information
  • We don’t use your info for ads or marketing

Where submissions go

Submissions may be sent to private moderation tools (for example, a private Discord mod channel) so staff can review them.

Retention

We keep submissions only as long as needed for moderation and server operations. If you want your submission removed, contact staff and we’ll do our best to help.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact server staff (for example via Discord) and include your Minecraft username.
