Privacy Policy

Last updated:

What we collect

We collect only the information you submit in the whitelist application form, such as your Minecraft username and how you found the server. Discord/Reddit/email are optional if you provide them.

How we use it

  • To review and approve/deny whitelist applications
  • To contact you if you provided contact info (optional)
  • To prevent spam/abuse (e.g., anti-bot checks)

What we don’t do

  • We don’t sell your information
  • We don’t run ads or share your details for marketing

Where submissions go

Submissions may be sent to our private moderation tools (for example, a private Discord mod channel) so staff can review them.

Retention

We keep submissions only as long as needed for moderation and server operations. If you want your submission removed, contact the server staff and we’ll do our best to help.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact server staff (e.g., via Discord) and include your Minecraft username.
