Privacy Policy

Last updated:

What we collect

We collect only what you submit in the whitelist application: your Minecraft username, and optionally your Discord username and how you found the server.

How we use it

  • To review and approve/deny whitelist applications
  • To contact/notify you if you provided a Discord username (optional)
  • To prevent spam/abuse using anti-bot verification (Turnstile)

What we don’t do

  • We don’t sell your information
  • We don’t use your information for advertising

Where submissions go

Submissions may be forwarded to private moderation tools (for example, a private Discord mod channel) for review.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact staff via Discord and include your Minecraft username.
